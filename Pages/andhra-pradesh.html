Hyderabadi Biryani

Aromatic rice dish with marinated meat, herbs, and spices.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, chicken/mutton, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, mint leaves, coriander leaves, spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf), saffron, ghee, oil.

Preparation: Marinate meat with yogurt and spices. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done. Layer partially cooked rice and marinated meat, then cook on low heat until fully done.

Pulihora

Tangy rice preparation made with tamarind and spices.

Ingredients: Rice, tamarind pulp, green chilies, red chilies, curry leaves, mustard seeds, turmeric, peanuts, chana dal, urad dal, asafoetida, oil.

Preparation: Cook rice and let it cool. Prepare tamarind pulp mixture with spices. Mix the cooled rice with the tamarind mixture and temper with spices.

Gongura Pachadi

Andhra-style chutney made with sorrel leaves and spices.

Ingredients: Gongura (sorrel) leaves, green chilies, red chilies, garlic, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, oil, salt.

Preparation: Fry gongura leaves and chilies. Grind the mixture with salt. Temper mustard seeds, garlic, and fenugreek seeds, then mix with the ground gongura.

Pesarattu

Green gram dosa served with ginger chutney.

Ingredients: Green gram (moong dal), rice, green chilies, ginger, cumin seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Soak green gram and rice overnight. Grind with chilies, ginger, and cumin to a smooth batter. Make dosas and serve with chutney.

Gutti Vankaya

Stuffed brinjal curry with a rich masala filling.

Ingredients: Small brinjals, peanuts, sesame seeds, coconut, tamarind, onions, garlic, red chilies, spices (coriander, cumin, turmeric), oil, salt.

Preparation: Roast and grind peanuts, sesame, coconut, and spices. Stuff the brinjals with this mixture and cook in a gravy made from onions and tamarind.

Pulasa Pulusu

Tangy fish curry made with Pulasa fish.

Ingredients: Pulasa fish, tamarind, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, turmeric, red chili powder, oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate fish with turmeric and salt. Cook onions and tomatoes with spices and tamarind. Add fish and simmer until done.

Bobbattu

Sweet flatbread stuffed with jaggery and chana dal.

Ingredients: Chana dal, jaggery, cardamom powder, wheat flour, ghee.

Preparation: Cook and mash chana dal with jaggery and cardamom. Prepare dough with wheat flour. Stuff dough with chana dal mixture and cook on a griddle with ghee.

Aratikaya Vepudu

Crispy fried plantain slices with spices.

Ingredients: Raw plantains, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, oil.

Preparation: Slice plantains thinly. Marinate with spices and fry until crispy.

Natu Kodi Koora

Country chicken curry with traditional spices.

Ingredients: Country chicken, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, oil, salt.

Preparation: Cook chicken with onions, tomatoes, and spices until tender.

Pootharekulu

Delicate sweet made with rice starch and sugar.

Ingredients: Rice starch, sugar, ghee.

Preparation: Prepare rice starch sheets. Spread sugar and ghee on sheets, roll them up and serve.
