Masala Dosa

Thin, crispy rice and lentil crepes filled with spiced potato filling.

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, potatoes, onions, mustard seeds, turmeric, curry leaves, oil.

Preparation: Make dosa batter, cook dosas, fill with spiced potato mixture.

Ragi Mudde

Steamed ragi (finger millet) balls, a staple in Karnataka.

Ingredients: Ragi flour, water.

Preparation: Cook ragi flour with water into a thick dough, shape into balls.

Akki Rotti

Rice flour flatbread with various additions like onions, coriander, and spices.

Ingredients: Rice flour, onions, coriander, green chilies, cumin seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Mix rice flour with vegetables, spices, and water, cook on a griddle.

Uppe Saaru

Spicy and tangy soup made with lentils and spices.

Ingredients: Toor dal, tamarind, tomatoes, garlic, red chilies, coriander, cumin, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Preparation: Cook lentils, tomatoes, and spices, temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Mangalorean Fish Curry

Spicy coconut-based curry with fish.

Ingredients: Fish, coconut, tamarind, red chilies, coriander, turmeric, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Preparation: Cook fish in coconut-tamarind gravy with spices.

Mysore Pak

Rich and decadent sweet made with gram flour, ghee, and sugar.

Ingredients: Gram flour, ghee, sugar.

Preparation: Cook gram flour in ghee, add sugar syrup, set into a sweet cake.

Chitranna

Lemon rice tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts.

Ingredients: Rice, lemon, mustard seeds, curry leaves, peanuts, turmeric, oil.

Preparation: Cook rice, temper with spices and lemon juice.

Rava Idli

Soft and fluffy steamed semolina cakes.

Ingredients: Semolina (rava), yogurt, baking soda, mustard seeds, curry leaves, cashews, oil.

Preparation: Make batter, steam in idli molds until cooked.
