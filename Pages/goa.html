Prawn Balagaanchi

Spicy prawn curry with coconut and tamarind.

Ingredients: Prawns, coconut milk, tamarind, red chilies, garlic, turmeric, coriander seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook prawns with coconut milk and tamarind, and add a spice paste made from red chilies, garlic, and coriander seeds.

Goan Fish Curry

Traditional Goan curry with coconut milk and spices.

Ingredients: Fish, coconut milk, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, ginger, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, tamarind, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook fish with a mixture of coconut milk, spices, and tamarind for a tangy and flavorful curry.

Bebinca

Layered Goan dessert made with coconut milk and jaggery.

Ingredients: Coconut milk, jaggery, eggs, flour, ghee, cardamom powder.

Preparation: Layer batter made with coconut milk, jaggery, and eggs, baking each layer separately until the dessert is set.

Sorpotel

Spicy pork curry traditionally served during festivals.

Ingredients: Pork, liver, vinegar, red chilies, garlic, ginger, cloves, cinnamon, peppercorns, onions, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook pork and liver with vinegar and spices to create a rich and spicy curry.

Chicken Xacuti

Chicken curry with roasted spices and coconut.

Ingredients: Chicken, coconut, onions, garlic, ginger, poppy seeds, red chilies, turmeric, coriander seeds, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook chicken with a spice paste made from roasted spices and coconut for a flavorful curry.

Goan Sausage Pulao

Rice dish with spicy Goan sausages and vegetables.

Ingredients: Goan sausages, rice, onions, tomatoes, green peas, potatoes, garlic, ginger, turmeric, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook rice with Goan sausages and vegetables for a hearty and spicy pulao.

Vindaloo

Spicy and tangy pork curry with vinegar and garlic.

Ingredients: Pork, vinegar, garlic, ginger, red chilies, turmeric, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, cinnamon, cloves, peppercorns, salt, oil.

Preparation: Marinate pork in vinegar and spices, then cook with a blend of garlic and red chilies for a fiery curry.

Goan Prawn Balchao

Prawns in a spicy, tangy, and sweet sauce.

Ingredients: Prawns, vinegar, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, red chilies, sugar, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook prawns in a tangy sauce made from vinegar, onions, tomatoes, and spices for a flavorful dish.

Prawn Risotto

Creamy rice dish with prawns and coconut milk.

Ingredients: Prawns, rice, coconut milk, onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, coriander leaves, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook rice with prawns and coconut milk for a creamy and flavorful risotto.

Kulkul

Sweet deep-fried Goan pastries.

Ingredients: Flour, coconut milk, sugar, ghee, salt, oil.

Preparation: Make a dough with flour, coconut milk, and sugar. Shape into small pastries and deep-fry until golden brown.
