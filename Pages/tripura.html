Berma

Smoked and fermented fish delicacy, a staple in Tripuri cuisine.

Ingredients: Freshwater fish, bamboo shoots, salt.

Preparation: Clean and marinate fish, smoke it over a fire, then ferment for several days.

Mosdeng Serma

Spicy chutney made with fermented fish and herbs.

Ingredients: Berma (fermented fish), onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, salt.

Preparation: Mix chopped onions, chilies, coriander with mashed berma, add salt to taste.

Wahan Muya

Bamboo shoot curry cooked with pork or fish.

Ingredients: Bamboo shoots, pork/fish, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, turmeric, spices.

Preparation: Cook bamboo shoots and meat with spices until tender.

Chak Hao Kheer

Black rice pudding made with milk and sugar.

Ingredients: Black rice, milk, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Cook black rice in milk until soft, add sugar, cardamom, and nuts.

Muya Bai Wahan

Vegetable curry made with fermented soybean paste and bamboo shoots.

Ingredients: Fermented soybean paste, bamboo shoots, onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, spices.

Preparation: Cook bamboo shoots with soybean paste, onions, and spices.

Bangui Rice

Flavored rice dish made with pork and spices.

Ingredients: Rice, pork, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, spices.

Preparation: Cook rice with spices, saut√© pork with onions, tomatoes, and spices, then mix with rice.

Maasor Tenga

Sour fish curry made with tomatoes and lemon.

Ingredients: Fish, tomatoes, lemon, green chilies, spices.

Preparation: Cook fish with tomatoes, lemon juice, chilies, and spices until done.

Pumpkin Eromba

Spicy pumpkin curry made with fermented fish or shrimp.

Ingredients: Pumpkin, fermented fish/shrimp, onions, garlic, ginger, spices.

Preparation: Cook pumpkin with fermented fish, onions, and spices until tender.

Pitha

Traditional rice cakes, both sweet and savory versions.
