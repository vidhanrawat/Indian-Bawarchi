Phagshapa

A traditional Sikkimese pork dish with radishes and dried chilies.

Ingredients: Pork, radish, dried chilies, ginger, garlic.

Preparation: Cook pork with radishes, dried chilies, ginger, and garlic.

Momos

Steamed dumplings with various fillings, served with dipping sauces.

Ingredients: Flour, vegetables/meat, spices.

Preparation: Fill dough with vegetables/meat, steam, serve with sauces.

Gundruk

Fermented leafy greens, a staple in Sikkimese cuisine.

Ingredients: Leafy greens, water, fermentation.

Preparation: Dry leafy greens, ferment, use in soups or as a side dish.

Kinema

Fermented soybean dish, often used in stews and curries.

Ingredients: Soybeans, water, fermentation.

Preparation: Soak and ferment soybeans, use in various dishes.

Sel Roti

Traditional Sikkimese rice flour bread, usually served during festivals.

Ingredients: Rice flour, sugar, water, oil for frying.

Preparation: Make a batter, fry in oil until crispy.

Chhurpi

Hard cheese made from yak's milk, used in various dishes.

Ingredients: Yak's milk, salt, fermentation.

Preparation: Curdle yak's milk, press into cheese, dry and ferment.

Chang

Fermented millet or barley beverage, a traditional Sikkimese drink.

Ingredients: Millet/barley, water, fermentation.

Preparation: Ferment millet or barley in water, strain, serve as a beverage.

Gundruk Soup

Soup made from fermented leafy greens (gundruk), usually with vegetables and spices.

Ingredients: Gundruk, vegetables, spices, water.

Preparation: Cook gundruk with vegetables, spices, and water to make a soup.
