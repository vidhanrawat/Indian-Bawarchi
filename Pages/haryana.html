Bajra Khichdi

Healthy porridge made from pearl millet and lentils.

Ingredients: Pearl millet (bajra), split green gram (moong dal), ghee, cumin seeds, asafoetida, turmeric, salt, water.

Preparation: Cook millet and lentils with spices in a pressure cooker until soft.

Haryana Kadhi

Yogurt-based curry with gram flour dumplings.

Ingredients: Yogurt, gram flour (besan), turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, garlic, green chilies, salt, oil.

Preparation: Prepare a yogurt-based curry with gram flour dumplings and temper with spices.

Besan Masala Roti

Spiced flatbread made from gram flour.

Ingredients: Gram flour (besan), whole wheat flour, green chilies, coriander leaves, turmeric, carom seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Knead dough with ingredients, roll into flatbreads, and cook on a griddle.

Bathua Raita

Yogurt-based side dish with chenopodium leaves.

Ingredients: Bathua (chenopodium) leaves, yogurt, cumin powder, salt, red chili powder.

Preparation: Boil and chop bathua leaves, mix with yogurt and spices.

Kachi Lassi

Refreshing buttermilk drink flavored with cumin and mint.

Ingredients: Buttermilk, roasted cumin powder, black salt, mint leaves.

Preparation: Mix buttermilk with spices and mint for a refreshing drink.

Bajre Ki Roti

Traditional flatbread made from pearl millet flour.

Ingredients: Pearl millet flour (bajra), salt, water, ghee.

Preparation: Knead flour with salt and water, roll into flatbreads, and cook on a griddle with ghee.

Churma

Sweet dish made from coarsely ground wheat flour and jaggery.

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, jaggery, ghee, cardamom powder.

Preparation: Cook wheat flour in ghee, mix with jaggery and cardamom.

Aloo Tikki

Spiced potato patties served with chutney.

Ingredients: Potatoes, green peas, green chilies, ginger, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Mash potatoes with spices, form into patties, and fry until crispy.

Mithe Chawal

Sweet rice dish flavored with saffron and cardamom.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, sugar, saffron, cardamom, ghee, nuts.

Preparation: Cook rice with sugar and spices, garnish with nuts.

Sangri Ki Sabzi

Desert bean stir-fry with spices.

Ingredients: Sangri beans, yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin seeds, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Soak sangri beans, cook with spices and yogurt.
