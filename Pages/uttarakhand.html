Aaloo ke Gutke

Spicy boiled potatoes sautéed with local spices.

Ingredients: Potatoes, mustard oil, cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander leaves.

Preparation: Boil potatoes, cut into pieces, sauté with spices until crispy.

Bhatt ki Churdkani

Black soybean curry cooked with local spices.

Ingredients: Black soybeans, mustard oil, cumin seeds, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, spices.

Preparation: Cook soaked soybeans with spices, onions, tomatoes until soft.

Jhangora Kheer

Millet pudding cooked with milk and sugar.

Ingredients: Jhangora (barnyard millet), milk, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Cook millet in milk, add sugar, cardamom, and nuts, simmer until thick.

Kafuli

Spinach curry cooked with local greens and spices.

Ingredients: Spinach, fenugreek leaves, mustard oil, cumin seeds, garlic, red chili powder, turmeric.

Preparation: Sauté greens with spices, cook until tender.

Phannu

Uttarakhandi-style lentil curry.

Ingredients: Urad dal, ghee, cumin seeds, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, spices.

Preparation: Cook urad dal with spices, temper with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Singal

Fried bread made with wheat flour.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, sugar, ghee, cardamom.

Preparation: Make dough, shape into flat discs, deep fry until golden, sprinkle with sugar.

Gulgula

Sweet fritters made with jaggery and whole wheat flour.

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, jaggery, fennel seeds, oil.

Preparation: Mix flour, jaggery, and fennel seeds into a batter, deep fry until golden.

Bhang ki Chutney

Chutney made with hemp seeds and spices.

Ingredients: Hemp seeds, tamarind, garlic, red chili powder, salt.

Preparation: Grind hemp seeds with spices and tamarind into a chutney.

Aaru ka Saag

Elephant foot yam curry cooked with local spices.

Ingredients: Elephant foot yam, mustard oil, cumin seeds, onions, garlic, spices.

Preparation: Cook yam with spices until soft.
