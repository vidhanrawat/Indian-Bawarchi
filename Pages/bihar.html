Litti Chokha

Roasted wheat balls stuffed with sattu and served with mashed vegetables.

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, sattu (roasted gram flour), onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, mustard oil, brinjal, tomatoes, potatoes, salt, spices.

Preparation: Prepare dough with wheat flour and water. Stuff with a mixture of sattu and spices. Roast litti balls and serve with chokha made from roasted brinjal, tomatoes, and potatoes.

Ghughni

Spicy yellow peas curry.

Ingredients: Yellow peas, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, cumin seeds, turmeric, garam masala, coriander leaves, oil, salt.

Preparation: Soak yellow peas overnight. Cook with spices, onions, and tomatoes until soft. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Thekua

Sweet fried cookies made with wheat flour and jaggery.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, jaggery, grated coconut, cardamom powder, ghee, oil.

Preparation: Mix wheat flour with jaggery, grated coconut, and cardamom powder. Shape into cookies and fry in ghee.

Chana Ghugni

Black chickpeas curry with spices.

Ingredients: Black chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, cumin seeds, turmeric, garam masala, coriander leaves, oil, salt.

Preparation: Soak black chickpeas overnight. Cook with spices, onions, and tomatoes until soft. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Malpua

Sweet pancakes soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Flour, milk, sugar, fennel seeds, cardamom powder, ghee.

Preparation: Make a batter with flour, milk, sugar, and fennel seeds. Fry small pancakes in ghee and soak in sugar syrup.

Khaja

Flaky pastry soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Flour, ghee, sugar, cardamom powder.

Preparation: Make dough with flour and ghee. Roll and fry to make layered pastries. Soak in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.

Dal Pitha

Steamed rice flour dumplings stuffed with spiced lentil mixture.

Ingredients: Rice flour, chana dal, onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, cumin seeds, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Make dough with rice flour and water. Fill with a mixture of cooked chana dal and spices. Steam until done.

Aloo Kachalu Chutney

Tangy potato salad with tamarind and spices.

Ingredients: Potatoes, tamarind pulp, green chilies, cumin seeds, coriander leaves, black salt, chaat masala.

Preparation: Boil and cube potatoes. Mix with tamarind pulp, chopped green chilies, cumin seeds, and spices.

Bihari Kebab

Spicy grilled meat skewers.

Ingredients: Meat (beef, lamb, or chicken), yogurt, onions, garlic, ginger, red chili powder, garam masala, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate meat with yogurt and spices. Skewer and grill until cooked through.

Bhojpuri Mutton

Hearty mutton curry with robust spices.

Ingredients: Mutton, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Cook mutton with onions, tomatoes, and spices until tender.
