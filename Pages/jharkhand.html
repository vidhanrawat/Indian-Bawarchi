Thekua

Deep-fried sweet made from wheat flour, jaggery, and coconut.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, jaggery, coconut, cardamom, ghee.

Preparation: Mix wheat flour, jaggery, coconut, and cardamom, deep-fry until golden brown.

Dhuska

Deep-fried rice and lentil pancakes.

Ingredients: Rice, chana dal, urad dal, cumin seeds, green chilies, ginger, salt, oil.

Preparation: Soak rice and dals, grind with spices, ferment, and deep-fry into pancakes.

Pittha

Steamed dumplings stuffed with spiced lentils or vegetables.

Ingredients: Rice flour, lentils/vegetables, spices, oil.

Preparation: Make dough with rice flour, stuff with spiced lentils or vegetables, steam until cooked.

Kodo Ko Roti

Millet flatbread made from kodo millet flour.

Ingredients: Kodo millet flour, water, salt, ghee.

Preparation: Mix kodo millet flour with water, make dough, roll into flatbreads, and cook on a griddle.

Sarso Saag

Mustard greens cooked with spices and served with makki ki roti.

Ingredients: Mustard greens, spinach, garlic, green chilies, spices, cornmeal, ghee.

Preparation: Cook greens with spices, serve with cornmeal flatbread.

Pitha

Traditional sweet or savory rice cakes.

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut, jaggery/sugar, sesame seeds, ghee.

Preparation: Make dough with rice flour, stuff with sweet or savory fillings, steam or deep-fry.

Chilka Roti

Rice flour flatbread with a crispy texture.

Ingredients: Rice flour, water, salt, ghee.

Preparation: Mix rice flour with water, roll into flatbreads, cook on a griddle until crispy.

Malpua

Deep-fried pancakes served with sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, milk, fennel seeds, cardamom, ghee.

Preparation: Make a batter with flour, sugar, and spices, deep-fry into pancakes, serve with syrup.

Daliya

Porridge made from cracked wheat or barley.

Ingredients: Cracked wheat/barley, milk, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Cook cracked wheat/barley with milk and sugar, garnish with cardamom and nuts.
