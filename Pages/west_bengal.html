Aloo Posto

Potatoes cooked in a poppy seed paste.

Ingredients: Potatoes, poppy seeds, mustard oil, green chilies, turmeric.

Preparation: Cook potatoes with poppy seed paste, chilies, and spices.

Shorshe Ilish

Hilsa fish cooked in a mustard sauce.

Ingredients: Hilsa fish, mustard paste, mustard oil, green chilies, turmeric.

Preparation: Marinate fish with mustard paste, cook in mustard oil with spices.

Machher Jhol

Spicy fish curry with vegetables.

Ingredients: Fish, onions, tomatoes, potatoes, mustard oil, spices.

Preparation: Cook fish with vegetables and spices until tender.

Bengali Biryani

Fragrant rice dish with meat and spices.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken/mutton), yogurt, onions, potatoes, spices.

Preparation: Marinate meat, cook rice, layer with meat, potatoes, and spices, then steam.

Roshogolla

Spongy cottage cheese balls in sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Cottage cheese (chenna), sugar, water, rose water.

Preparation: Knead chenna, shape into balls, boil in sugar syrup with rose water.

Doi Maach

Fish curry cooked in a yogurt-based gravy.

Ingredients: Fish, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, spices.

Preparation: Cook fish in yogurt-based gravy with spices.

Bengali Samosa

Deep-fried pastry with spiced potato filling.

Ingredients: Flour, potatoes, green chilies, spices, oil.

Preparation: Make dough, fill with spiced potatoes, shape into triangles, deep fry.

Bhapa Iliish

Steamed Hilsa fish marinated in mustard paste.

Ingredients: Hilsa fish, mustard paste, green chilies, turmeric, mustard oil.

Preparation: Marinate fish with mustard paste, steam until cooked.

Kolkata Biryani

Spicy rice dish with meat, potatoes, and boiled eggs.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken/mutton), potatoes, boiled eggs, yogurt, spices.

Preparation: Cook rice, layer with cooked meat, potatoes, eggs, and spices, then steam.
