Vada Pav

Spicy potato fritter sandwiched in a pav (bread) with chutneys.

Ingredients: Potatoes, gram flour, pav bread, garlic chutney, tamarind chutney, spices.

Preparation: Make potato fritters, assemble in pav with chutneys.

Pav Bhaji

Mixed vegetable curry served with buttered pav (bread).

Ingredients: Potatoes, peas, onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, pav bread, butter, spices.

Preparation: Cook vegetables, mash, serve with buttered pav.

Misal Pav

Spicy sprout curry served with pav and farsan (crispy mix).

Ingredients: Mixed sprouts, onions, tomatoes, farsan, pav bread, spices.

Preparation: Cook sprouts, serve with pav, and garnish with farsan.

Puran Poli

Sweet flatbread stuffed with a mixture of lentils, jaggery, and spices.

Ingredients: Chana dal, jaggery, cardamom, flour, ghee.

Preparation: Make stuffing, stuff in dough, cook with ghee.

Bharli Vangi

Stuffed baby eggplants cooked in a spicy masala.

Ingredients: Baby eggplants, coconut, peanuts, spices.

Preparation: Stuff eggplants with masala, cook until tender.

Sabudana Khichdi

Tapioca pearls cooked with peanuts, potatoes, and spices.

Ingredients: Sabudana (tapioca pearls), peanuts, potatoes, spices.

Preparation: Soak sabudana, cook with potatoes, peanuts, and spices.

Kothimbir Vadi

Coriander and gram flour fritters.

Ingredients: Coriander leaves, gram flour, spices.

Preparation: Make batter, steam, slice, and shallow fry.

Modak

Steamed sweet dumplings filled with coconut and jaggery.

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut, jaggery, cardamom.

Preparation: Make dough, fill with coconut-jaggery mixture, steam.

Shrikhand

Sweet strained yogurt flavored with saffron and cardamom.

Ingredients: Yogurt, sugar, saffron, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Strain yogurt, mix with sugar and flavors, chill.

Kande Pohe

Flattened rice cooked with onions and spices.

Ingredients: Poha (flattened rice), onions, mustard seeds, turmeric, curry leaves.
