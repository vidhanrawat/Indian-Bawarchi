Idli Sambar

Steamed rice cakes (idli) served with lentil and vegetable stew (sambar).

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, vegetables, tamarind, spices.

Preparation: Ferment rice and lentils, steam to make idlis. Cook lentils and vegetables with tamarind and spices to make sambar.

Dosa

Thin, crispy pancakes made from fermented rice and lentil batter.

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, fenugreek seeds, salt.

Preparation: Ferment rice, lentils, and fenugreek seeds, spread batter on a hot griddle to make dosas.

Pongal

A savory rice and lentil dish, often served with chutney.

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, ghee, pepper, cumin, curry leaves.

Preparation: Cook rice and lentils until soft, temper with spices and ghee.

Sambhar

Spicy lentil and vegetable stew, often served with rice or idli.

Ingredients: Lentils, vegetables, tamarind, spices, curry leaves.

Preparation: Cook lentils and vegetables with tamarind and spices to make sambhar.

Rasam

Spicy and tangy soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients: Tamarind, tomatoes, spices (mustard seeds, cumin, pepper, coriander), curry leaves.

Preparation: Extract tamarind juice, cook with tomatoes and spices to make rasam.

Bisi Bele Bath

Spicy rice and lentil dish cooked with vegetables and spices.

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, vegetables, tamarind, spices (cinnamon, cloves, cumin, coriander).

Preparation: Cook rice and lentils, saut√© vegetables, mix with tamarind and spices.

Thayir Sadam

Curd rice, a simple and popular dish made with yogurt and rice.

Ingredients: Rice, yogurt, mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies.

Preparation: Cook rice, mix with yogurt, temper with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies.

Vada

Deep-fried lentil fritters, often served with chutney or sambar.

Ingredients: Lentils, onions, green chilies, curry leaves, spices.

Preparation: Grind lentils, mix with spices and onions, shape into fritters, deep fry until golden.

Payasam

Rich and creamy milk-based dessert with rice, vermicelli, or lentils.

Ingredients: Milk, rice/vermicelli/lentils, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Cook main ingredient in milk, add sugar, cardamom, and nuts.
