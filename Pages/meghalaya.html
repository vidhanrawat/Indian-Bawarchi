Jadoh

Flavored rice dish with meat (usually pork) and spices.

Ingredients: Rice, pork, ginger, garlic, onion, turmeric, black pepper, bay leaves.

Preparation: Cook rice with spices. Separately, cook pork with spices, then mix with cooked rice.

Doh Nei-Iong

Pork cooked in black sesame paste.

Ingredients: Pork, black sesame seeds, ginger, garlic, onion, chili, salt.

Preparation: Roast sesame seeds, grind into paste. Cook pork with sesame paste and spices.

Pukhlein

Sweet rice cake made with rice flour and jaggery.

Ingredients: Rice flour, jaggery, coconut, cardamom, oil.

Preparation: Mix rice flour, jaggery, and coconut. Shape into cakes and fry.

Tungrymbai

Fermented soybean dish with pork or fish.

Ingredients: Fermented soybeans, pork or fish, ginger, garlic, onion, chili.

Preparation: Cook fermented soybeans with pork/fish and spices.

Pyrku

Spicy chutney made with fermented fish.

Ingredients: Fermented fish, chili, onion, garlic, ginger, salt.

Preparation: Grind fermented fish with spices into a chutney.

Doh Khlieh

Salad made with minced pork, onions, and spices.

Ingredients: Minced pork, onions, chili, lime juice, salt.

Preparation: Mix minced pork with chopped onions, chili, lime juice, and salt.

Ja Stew

Chicken stew with potatoes and vegetables.

Ingredients: Chicken, potatoes, carrots, beans, spices, coconut milk.

Preparation: Cook chicken with vegetables, spices, and coconut milk until tender.

Do Ondo

Rice dumplings cooked with meat or vegetables.

Ingredients: Rice flour, meat or vegetables, spices.

Preparation: Shape rice flour dough into dumplings, cook with meat/vegetables and spices.

Doh Jem

Fried pork fat with spices.

Ingredients: Pork fat, chili, ginger, garlic, salt.

Preparation: Fry pork fat with spices until crispy.

Putharo

Steamed rice cakes served with pork or chicken curry.

Ingredients: Rice, pork or chicken, spices.

Preparation: Steam rice cakes, serve with curry.
