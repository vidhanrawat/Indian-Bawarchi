Hyderabadi Biryani

Aromatic rice dish with marinated meat, herbs, and spices.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, chicken/mutton, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, mint leaves, coriander leaves, spices, saffron, ghee, oil.

Preparation: Marinate meat with yogurt and spices. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done. Layer partially cooked rice and marinated meat, then cook on low heat until fully done.

Gongura Mutton

Spicy and tangy mutton curry made with gongura leaves.

Ingredients: Mutton, gongura leaves, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, spices, oil.

Preparation: Cook mutton with gongura leaves, onions, tomatoes, and spices until tender.

Pachi Pulusu

Traditional sour and spicy tamarind soup with vegetables.

Ingredients: Tamarind, vegetables (like brinjal, tomato, okra), green chilies, onions, garlic, mustard seeds, spices.

Preparation: Extract tamarind juice, mix with chopped vegetables, temper with mustard seeds, garlic, and spices.

Pootharekulu

Delicate sweet made with rice starch and sugar.

Ingredients: Rice starch, sugar, ghee.

Preparation: Prepare rice starch sheets. Spread sugar and ghee on sheets, roll them up and serve.

Kodi Iguru

Spicy chicken curry cooked with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients: Chicken, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, spices, curry leaves, oil.

Preparation: Cook chicken with onions, tomatoes, chilies, and spices until well cooked.

Ariselu

Deep-fried sweet made with rice flour, jaggery, and sesame seeds.

Ingredients: Rice flour, jaggery, sesame seeds, ghee.

Preparation: Make a dough with rice flour, jaggery, and sesame seeds, flatten and deep fry until golden.

Makka Roti

Flatbread made from cornmeal, typically served with spicy curries.

Ingredients: Cornmeal, water, salt, oil.

Preparation: Knead cornmeal with water and salt, flatten into rotis, cook on a griddle with oil.

Kobbari Karam

Coconut chutney made with roasted spices and herbs.

Ingredients: Coconut, dried red chilies, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, tamarind, salt.

Preparation: Roast spices, grind with coconut, tamarind, and salt to make a chutney.
