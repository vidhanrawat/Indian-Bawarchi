Sarovar Dal

Creamy lentil dish tempered with spices and ghee.

Ingredients: Lentils, spices, ghee, cream.

Preparation: Cook lentils, temper with spices, ghee, and finish with cream.

Butter Chicken

Chicken cooked in a rich, buttery tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients: Chicken, tomatoes, butter, cream, spices.

Preparation: Cook chicken in tomato-based sauce with butter, cream, and spices.

Sarson Da Saag

Mustard greens cooked with spices and served with makki di roti.

Ingredients: Mustard greens, spices, cornmeal (makki atta).

Preparation: Cook mustard greens with spices, serve with cornmeal flatbread.

Chana Masala

Spicy chickpea curry with a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, spices, ginger, garlic.

Preparation: Cook chickpeas with onions, tomatoes, spices, ginger, and garlic.

Paneer Tikka

Marinated paneer (Indian cheese) skewers grilled to perfection.

Ingredients: Paneer, yogurt, spices, bell peppers, onions.

Preparation: Marinate paneer with yogurt and spices, skewer with veggies, grill until charred.

Aaloo Gobi

Classic potato and cauliflower curry with spices.

Ingredients: Potatoes, cauliflower, spices, onions, tomatoes.

Preparation: Cook potatoes and cauliflower with spices, onions, and tomatoes.

Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in yogurt and tandoori spices, roasted in a tandoor oven.

Ingredients: Chicken, yogurt, tandoori spices.

Preparation: Marinate chicken with yogurt and tandoori spices, roast in a tandoor oven or grill.

Lassi

Refreshing yogurt-based drink, sweet or salty.

Ingredients: Yogurt, sugar/salt, water, spices (optional).

Preparation: Blend yogurt with sugar/salt, water, and spices if using, serve chilled.

Rajma Chawal

Red kidney beans curry served with steamed rice.

Ingredients: Kidney beans, rice, onions, tomatoes, spices.

Preparation: Cook kidney beans with onions, tomatoes, spices, serve with steamed rice.
