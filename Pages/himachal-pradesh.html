Madra

Chickpea-based curry with yogurt and spices.

Ingredients: Chickpeas, yogurt, gram flour, ghee, cumin seeds, asafoetida, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf, cardamom, turmeric, salt.

Preparation: Cook chickpeas with yogurt and spices in ghee.

Dhaam

Traditional Himachali feast with rice, dal, and vegetables.

Ingredients: Rice, rajma, moong dal, yogurt, boondi, kadhi, vegetables, spices.

Preparation: Prepare various dishes like rajma, moong dal, and kadhi, and serve together with rice and boondi raita.

Siddu

Steamed bread stuffed with spiced mixture.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, yeast, poppy seeds, walnuts, spices, ghee.

Preparation: Make dough with yeast, stuff with spiced mixture, and steam until cooked.

Chana Madra

Yogurt-based curry with chickpeas.

Ingredients: Chickpeas, yogurt, gram flour, cumin seeds, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, bay leaf, turmeric, salt, ghee.

Preparation: Cook chickpeas with yogurt and spices in ghee.

Babru

Deep-fried bread stuffed with black gram paste.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, black gram dal, spices, oil.

Preparation: Stuff wheat flour dough with spiced black gram paste and deep fry.

Aktori

Buckwheat pancakes popular in Himachal Pradesh.

Ingredients: Buckwheat flour, wheat flour, milk, sugar, ghee.

Preparation: Make a batter with buckwheat and wheat flour, fry pancakes in ghee.

Chana Dal

Split chickpea lentils cooked with spices.

Ingredients: Chana dal, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, cumin seeds, turmeric, coriander powder, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook chana dal with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Mittha

Sweet dish made from rice, dry fruits, and sugar.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, sugar, dry fruits (raisins, almonds, cashews), cardamom, ghee.

Preparation: Cook rice with sugar and cardamom, garnish with dry fruits.

Kullu Trout

Grilled trout fish with minimal spices.

Ingredients: Trout fish, lemon, spices (salt, pepper, turmeric), mustard oil.

Preparation: Marinate trout with spices and lemon, grill or bake until cooked.

Patande

Himachali pancakes served with ghee and sugar.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, milk, sugar, ghee.

Preparation: Make a batter with wheat flour and milk, fry pancakes in ghee.
