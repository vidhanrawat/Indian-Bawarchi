Dalma

Mixed lentil and vegetable stew, a staple in Odia cuisine.

Ingredients: Lentils, vegetables (pumpkin, potato, etc.), spices, ghee.

Preparation: Cook lentils and vegetables together with spices, temper with ghee.

Pakhala

Fermented rice soaked in water, typically served with fried or curried fish.

Ingredients: Fermented rice, water, fish, spices.

Preparation: Soak fermented rice in water, serve with fish curry or fried fish.

Chhena Poda

Baked cottage cheese dessert, sweet and caramelized.

Ingredients: Cottage cheese, sugar, cardamom, ghee.

Preparation: Mix cottage cheese with sugar, cardamom, shape into a cake, and bake until golden.

Machha Besara

Fish curry cooked with mustard paste and spices.

Ingredients: Fish, mustard paste, spices, tomatoes, onions.

Preparation: Cook fish with mustard paste, spices, tomatoes, and onions.

Bhindi Bhaja

Okra slices marinated and fried until crispy.

Ingredients: Okra, spices, oil.

Preparation: Marinate okra slices with spices, fry until crispy.

Enduri Pitha

Steamed rice cake stuffed with coconut and jaggery.

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut, jaggery, banana leaves.

Preparation: Make rice cake dough, stuff with coconut-jaggery mix, wrap in banana leaves, and steam.

Santula

Mixed vegetable curry with minimal spices, light and healthy.

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, panch phoron (five-spice mix), turmeric, mustard oil.

Preparation: Cook mixed vegetables with panch phoron, turmeric, and mustard oil.
