Poha

Flattened rice flakes cooked with onions, potatoes, and spices.

Ingredients: Poha (flattened rice), onions, potatoes, peanuts, turmeric, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Preparation: Rinse poha, sauté with onions, potatoes, and spices.

Bhopali Gosht Korma

Creamy and aromatic mutton curry from Bhopal.

Ingredients: Mutton, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, cashews, spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon), ghee.

Preparation: Cook mutton with spices, yogurt, and cashew paste until tender.

Bhutte Ki Kees

Grated corn cooked with spices and milk.

Ingredients: Grated corn, milk, ginger, green chilies, mustard seeds, turmeric, ghee.

Preparation: Sauté corn with spices and milk until cooked.

Dal Bafla

Steamed wheat and lentil dumplings served with dal.

Ingredients: Wheat flour, toor dal, spices (cumin, coriander, chili), ghee.

Preparation: Make dough, shape into balls, steam, and serve with dal.

Mawa Bati

Sweet balls made from mawa (milk solids), sugar, and nuts.

Ingredients: Mawa, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Mix mawa with sugar, shape into balls, garnish with nuts.

Bhutte Ka Kebab

Corn and potato kebabs flavored with spices.

Ingredients: Corn kernels, potatoes, bread crumbs, spices (ginger, chili, coriander), oil.

Preparation: Mash corn and potatoes, mix with spices, shape into kebabs, and grill.

Shikanji

Refreshing lemonade with spices like cumin and black salt.

Ingredients: Lemon juice, water, sugar, cumin powder, black salt, mint leaves.

Preparation: Mix lemon juice with water, sugar, and spices, garnish with mint.

Malpua

Deep-fried pancakes soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Flour, milk, sugar, fennel seeds, cardamom, ghee.

Preparation: Make a batter, deep-fry into pancakes, soak in sugar syrup.

Khasta Kachori

Crunchy deep-fried pastry filled with spiced lentils.

Ingredients: Flour, urad dal, spices (fennel, coriander, chili), ghee, oil.

Preparation: Make dough, fill with spiced lentils, deep-fry until golden.
