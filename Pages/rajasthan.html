Dal Bati Churma

Three-part meal: lentils (dal), baked wheat balls (bati), and sweet crumbled bread (churma).

Ingredients: Lentils, wheat flour, ghee, sugar, spices.

Preparation: Cook lentils, bake wheat balls, crush and mix sweet bread.

Gatte ki Sabji

Gram flour (besan) dumplings in spicy yogurt-based curry.

Ingredients: Gram flour, yogurt, spices, oil.

Preparation: Make gram flour dumplings, cook in spicy yogurt curry.

Ker Sangri

A traditional Rajasthani dish made with dried desert beans (ker) and dried berries (saangri).

Ingredients: Ker (dried beans), saangri (dried berries), spices, oil.

Preparation: Soak and cook dried beans and berries with spices.

Laal Maas

Spicy mutton curry cooked with red chilies and yogurt.

Ingredients: Mutton, red chilies, yogurt, spices, ghee.

Preparation: Cook mutton with red chilies, yogurt, and spices.

Papad ki Sabji

Crispy papads cooked in a spicy gravy.

Ingredients: Papads, spices, tomatoes, onions.

Preparation: Break papads, cook in spicy gravy with tomatoes and onions.

Ghevar

Deep-fried sweet made from flour, soaked in sugar syrup, and topped with almonds.

Ingredients: Flour, ghee, sugar syrup, almonds.

Preparation: Fry flour batter, soak in sugar syrup, garnish with almonds.

Mawa Kachori

Deep-fried pastry filled with sweetened condensed milk (mawa) and nuts.

Ingredients: Pastry dough, mawa, nuts, sugar syrup.

Preparation: Fill pastry with mawa and nuts, deep fry, soak in sugar syrup.

Pyaaz Kachori

Deep-fried pastry filled with spiced onion mixture.

Ingredients: Pastry dough, onions, spices, oil.

Preparation: Fill pastry with spiced onion mixture, deep fry until golden.

Dal Baati Churma

Similar to Dal Bati Churma, but with baked wheat balls (baati) instead of crumbled bread.

Ingredients: Lentils, wheat flour, ghee, sugar, spices.

Preparation: Cook lentils, bake wheat balls, serve with sweet churma.

Mohan Thaal

A rich, sweet dessert made with gram flour, ghee, sugar, and nuts.
