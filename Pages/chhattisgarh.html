Chila

Savory pancakes made with rice flour and chana dal.

Ingredients: Rice flour, chana dal, onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Make a batter with rice flour, soaked and ground chana dal, and spices. Cook on a griddle until golden brown.

Fara

Steamed dumplings made from rice flour with spicy lentil filling.

Ingredients: Rice flour, chana dal, green chilies, ginger, garlic, cumin seeds, coriander leaves, salt.

Preparation: Prepare dough with rice flour and water. Fill with a mixture of cooked chana dal and spices. Steam until done.

Bafauri

Healthy steamed lentil balls.

Ingredients: Chana dal, onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, turmeric, salt.

Preparation: Soak chana dal and grind with spices. Shape into balls and steam until cooked.

Angakar Roti

Traditional rice flour roti.

Ingredients: Rice flour, water, salt, oil.

Preparation: Make a dough with rice flour, water, and salt. Roll into rotis and cook on a griddle until done.

Dehrori

Sweet rice dumplings soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Rice, curd, sugar, cardamom powder, ghee, oil.

Preparation: Ferment rice and curd mixture overnight. Fry dumplings and soak in sugar syrup.

Dubki Kadhi

Yogurt-based curry with gram flour dumplings.

Ingredients: Gram flour, yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, salt.

Preparation: Prepare gram flour dumplings and fry them. Make a curry with yogurt and spices, and add the dumplings.

Tilgur

Sesame and jaggery sweets.

Ingredients: Sesame seeds, jaggery, cardamom powder.

Preparation: Roast sesame seeds. Melt jaggery and mix with sesame seeds. Shape into small balls or bars.

Chousela

Deep-fried rice flour puris.

Ingredients: Rice flour, water, salt, oil.

Preparation: Make a dough with rice flour and water. Roll into small puris and deep-fry until golden.

Muthia

Steamed dumplings made with gram flour and spices.

Ingredients: Gram flour, fenugreek leaves, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, oil, salt.

Preparation: Mix gram flour with spices and fenugreek leaves. Shape into logs and steam. Slice and temper with spices.

Kusli

Sweet deep-fried pastries with a coconut filling.

Ingredients: Flour, coconut, jaggery, cardamom powder, ghee, oil.

Preparation: Make dough with flour and ghee. Fill with a mixture of coconut and jaggery. Shape into pastries and deep-fry.
