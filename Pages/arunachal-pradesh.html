Thukpa

Nourishing noodle soup with vegetables and meat.

Ingredients: Noodles, chicken/vegetables, garlic, ginger, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, soy sauce, salt, oil, spices.

Preparation: Boil noodles until cooked. Sauté garlic, ginger, onions, and tomatoes with spices. Add chicken/vegetables and cook. Add cooked noodles to the broth and simmer.

Momos

Steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.

Ingredients: Flour, water, minced meat/vegetables, onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, salt, oil.

Preparation: Prepare dough with flour and water. Mix minced meat/vegetables with onions, garlic, ginger, and spices. Fill dough with mixture and steam until cooked.

Nga Thongba

Spicy fish stew with local herbs and spices.

Ingredients: Fish, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, green chilies, turmeric, coriander, local herbs, oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate fish with turmeric and salt. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices. Add fish and simmer with local herbs until done.

Apong

Traditional rice beer.

Ingredients: Fermented rice, water.

Preparation: Ferment rice with water for several days. Strain the mixture and serve.

Bamboo Shoots

Bamboo shoot stir-fry with local spices.

Ingredients: Bamboo shoots, garlic, ginger, green chilies, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Slice bamboo shoots and boil. Sauté with garlic, ginger, green chilies, and mustard oil.

Lukter

Smoked meat with dry red chili flakes.

Ingredients: Smoked meat, dry red chili flakes, salt, oil.

Preparation: Sauté smoked meat with dry red chili flakes and salt.

Pika Pila

Traditional pickle made with bamboo shoots and pork fat.

Ingredients: Bamboo shoots, pork fat, spices, oil, salt.

Preparation: Cook bamboo shoots and pork fat with spices until tender. Store in oil and serve as a pickle.

Zan

Thick porridge made from millet flour.

Ingredients: Millet flour, water, vegetables, spices.

Preparation: Cook millet flour with water and vegetables until thickened. Season with spices.

Chura Sabzi

Fermented cheese curry with vegetables.

Ingredients: Fermented cheese, vegetables, garlic, ginger, green chilies, spices, salt.

Preparation: Cook fermented cheese with vegetables, garlic, ginger, and spices until well blended.

Pasa

Fish soup with local herbs and spices.

Ingredients: Fish, local herbs, garlic, ginger, green chilies, spices, salt.

Preparation: Cook fish with garlic, ginger, green chilies, local herbs, and spices until done.
