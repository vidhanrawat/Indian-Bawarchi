Masor Tenga

Tangy fish curry with tomatoes and lemon.

Ingredients: Fish, tomatoes, lemon, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, green chilies, turmeric, oil, salt.

Preparation: Fry mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add fish and cook with turmeric and green chilies. Finish with lemon juice.

Assamese Duck Curry

Rich duck curry with ash gourd and spices.

Ingredients: Duck, ash gourd, onions, ginger, garlic, black pepper, cardamom, cloves, bay leaves, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate duck with spices. Sauté onions, ginger, and garlic. Add duck and cook until browned. Add ash gourd and cook until tender.

Khaar

Traditional Assamese dish made with raw papaya and alkali.

Ingredients: Raw papaya, pigeon meat (optional), pulses, mustard oil, khaar (alkaline extract), salt.

Preparation: Cook raw papaya with pulses and pigeon meat. Add khaar and simmer until done.

Pithas

Assamese rice cakes with sweet or savory filling.

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut, jaggery, sesame seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Prepare dough with rice flour and water. Fill with coconut and jaggery mixture. Steam or fry until done.

Paror Mangxo

Pigeon meat curry with local spices.

Ingredients: Pigeon meat, onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, turmeric, coriander, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate pigeon meat with turmeric and salt. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger. Add pigeon meat and cook with spices until tender.

Aaloo Pitika

Mashed potatoes with mustard oil and green chilies.

Ingredients: Potatoes, green chilies, onions, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Boil and mash potatoes. Mix with chopped onions, green chilies, mustard oil, and salt.

Pork with Bamboo Shoot

Flavorful pork curry with bamboo shoots.

Ingredients: Pork, bamboo shoots, ginger, garlic, green chilies, turmeric, mustard oil, salt.

Preparation: Marinate pork with turmeric and salt. Cook pork with ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Add bamboo shoots and simmer until done.

Payas

Sweet rice pudding with milk and jaggery.

Ingredients: Rice, milk, jaggery, cardamom, nuts, ghee.

Preparation: Cook rice in milk until soft. Add jaggery and cardamom. Garnish with nuts and ghee.

Fish Tenga

Light fish curry with tomatoes and lemon juice.

Ingredients: Fish, tomatoes, lemon, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, green chilies, turmeric, oil, salt.

Preparation: Fry mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add fish and cook with turmeric and green chilies. Finish with lemon juice.

Laru

Sweet coconut balls made with jaggery and coconut.

Ingredients: Grated coconut, jaggery, cardamom powder.

Preparation: Cook grated coconut with jaggery until thickened. Shape into small balls and sprinkle with cardamom powder.
