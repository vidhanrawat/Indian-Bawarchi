Rogan Josh

Flavorful lamb curry cooked with spices like fennel and Kashmiri red chili.

Ingredients: Lamb, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, spices (fennel, Kashmiri red chili, cardamom, cinnamon), ghee/oil.

Preparation: Marinate lamb, cook with spices and yogurt until tender.

Gushtaba

Minced mutton meatballs cooked in yogurt gravy.

Ingredients: Minced mutton, yogurt, spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon), ghee, milk.

Preparation: Make meatballs, cook in yogurt gravy with spices.

Yakhni

Yogurt-based curry with meat or vegetables.

Ingredients: Meat/vegetables, yogurt, spices (fennel, cloves, cardamom), ghee, mint leaves.

Preparation: Cook meat/vegetables in yogurt gravy with spices.

Roth

Sweet bread made with rice flour, ghee, and sugar.

Ingredients: Rice flour, ghee, sugar, cardamom, almonds.

Preparation: Knead dough, shape into discs, cook with ghee, and coat with sugar.

Kahwa

Traditional Kashmiri green tea infused with spices.

Ingredients: Green tea leaves, saffron, cardamom, cinnamon, almonds, honey.

Preparation: Brew green tea with spices, garnish with almonds and saffron, sweeten with honey.

Dum Aloo

Potatoes cooked in a spicy yogurt-based gravy.

Ingredients: Potatoes, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, spices (Kashmiri red chili, fennel, cinnamon), ghee/oil.

Preparation: Cook potatoes in yogurt gravy with spices until tender.

Shufta

Rich dry fruit dessert with saffron and spices.

Ingredients: Almonds, cashews, raisins, coconut, saffron, cardamom, sugar.

Preparation: Roast dry fruits, mix with saffron and sugar syrup, garnish with coconut.

Tabak Maaz

Deep-fried lamb ribs cooked with spices.

Ingredients: Lamb ribs, yogurt, spices (ginger, fennel, Kashmiri red chili), ghee/oil.

Preparation: Marinate ribs, deep-fry, then cook in yogurt gravy with spices.

Modur Pulao

Sweet saffron-flavored rice.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, saffron, ghee, nuts, sugar.

Preparation: Cook rice with saffron, sugar, and nuts in ghee.
