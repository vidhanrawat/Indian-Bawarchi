Tunday Kebab

Spicy and flavorful kebabs made from minced meat and spices.

Ingredients: Minced meat (usually lamb), spices, yogurt, onions, herbs.

Preparation: Marinate meat with spices and yogurt, skewer, and grill until cooked.

Chhole Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, spices, flour, yogurt.

Preparation: Cook chickpeas with onions, tomatoes, and spices. Make a dough for bhature, deep fry.

Aloo Tikki

Spiced potato patties served with chutneys and yogurt.

Ingredients: Potatoes, spices, breadcrumbs, oil.

Preparation: Mash potatoes, add spices and breadcrumbs, shape into patties, shallow fry.

Dum Ka Murgh

Slow-cooked chicken curry with yogurt and spices.

Ingredients: Chicken, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, spices, ghee.

Preparation: Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices, slow cook with onions, tomatoes, and ghee.

Samosa

Deep-fried pastry with spiced potato or meat filling.

Ingredients: Flour, potatoes/meat, spices, oil.

Preparation: Make dough, fill with spiced filling, shape into triangles, deep fry.

Shahi Paneer

Rich and creamy paneer (cottage cheese) curry.

Ingredients: Paneer, cream, tomatoes, onions, cashews, spices.

Preparation: Cook paneer in a creamy tomato-cashew sauce with spices.

Petha

Translucent soft candy made from ash gourd and sugar.

Ingredients: Ash gourd, sugar, water, flavorings (like rose water).

Preparation: Cook ash gourd with sugar and flavorings until translucent, then shape into candy.

Dahi Puri

Small crispy puris filled with spiced potatoes, chutneys, and yogurt.

Ingredients: Puris, potatoes, yogurt, chutneys, spices.

Preparation: Fill puris with potatoes, chutneys, and yogurt, serve immediately.

Kachori

Deep-fried pastry filled with spiced lentils or peas.

Ingredients: Flour, lentils/peas, spices, oil.

Preparation: Make dough, fill with spiced lentils/peas, shape into rounds, deep fry.
