Appam

Lacy fermented rice pancakes, a Kerala breakfast staple.

Ingredients: Rice, coconut, yeast, sugar, salt.

Preparation: Ferment rice, coconut, and yeast, cook into thin pancakes.

Puttu

Steamed rice cakes layered with coconut, often served with kadala curry.

Ingredients: Rice flour, grated coconut, salt.

Preparation: Layer rice flour and coconut, steam until cooked.

Fish Molee

Coconut milk-based fish stew with spices and curry leaves.

Ingredients: Fish, coconut milk, onions, tomatoes, ginger, green chilies, turmeric, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Preparation: Cook fish in coconut milk with spices and tempering.

Avial

Mixed vegetable curry with coconut, yogurt, and curry leaves.

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, coconut, yogurt, green chilies, turmeric, curry leaves, coconut oil.

Preparation: Cook vegetables with coconut, yogurt, and spices, temper with curry leaves.

Kerala Beef Fry

Spicy and flavorful beef fry cooked with onions and spices.

Ingredients: Beef, onions, ginger-garlic paste, spices (black pepper, coriander, turmeric), curry leaves, coconut oil.

Preparation: Marinate beef, fry with onions and spices until tender.

Karimeen Pollichathu

Pearl spot fish marinated in spices, wrapped in banana leaf, and grilled.

Ingredients: Pearl spot fish, spices (chili, turmeric, pepper), ginger-garlic, curry leaves, coconut oil.

Preparation: Marinate fish, wrap in banana leaf, grill until cooked.

Sadya

Traditional Kerala feast with a variety of vegetarian dishes served on a banana leaf.

Ingredients: Various vegetables, coconut, spices, rice, lentils, banana leaf.

Preparation: Cook and serve an assortment of dishes on a banana leaf.

Elaneer Payasam

Tender coconut kheer made with coconut water, milk, and jaggery.

Ingredients: Tender coconut water, milk, jaggery, rice, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Cook rice in coconut water and milk, sweeten with jaggery, garnish with nuts.
