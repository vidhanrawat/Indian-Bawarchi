Dhokla

Steamed savory cake made from fermented rice and chickpea batter.

Ingredients: Gram flour (besan), yogurt, green chilies, ginger, lemon juice, fruit salt, mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, coriander leaves, sugar, salt, oil.

Preparation: Prepare batter with gram flour and yogurt, ferment it, steam it, and then temper with spices.

Khandvi

Delicate rolls made from chickpea flour and yogurt.

Ingredients: Gram flour (besan), yogurt, turmeric, ginger paste, green chili paste, mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, coriander leaves, grated coconut, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook a batter made from gram flour and yogurt, spread thin, cut into strips, roll, and temper with spices.

Undhiyu

Mixed vegetable dish cooked with special spices and herbs.

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables (eggplant, potatoes, sweet potatoes, green beans, green peas), methi muthia, coconut, coriander leaves, green chilies, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds, carom seeds, turmeric, cumin, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook mixed vegetables and methi muthia with a blend of spices and herbs.

Thepla

Spiced flatbread made with wheat flour and fenugreek leaves.

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, fenugreek leaves, yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, sesame seeds, cumin seeds, salt, oil.

Preparation: Knead dough with ingredients, roll into flatbreads, and cook on a griddle.

Fafda Jalebi

Crunchy chickpea flour snack served with sweet syrupy jalebi.

Ingredients: For Fafda: Gram flour (besan), carom seeds, turmeric, salt, oil. For Jalebi: All-purpose flour, yogurt, saffron, sugar, ghee.

Preparation: Deep fry fafda made from gram flour dough and serve with syrupy jalebi.

Handvo

Savory cake made with mixed lentils and rice, spiced with ginger and green chilies.

Ingredients: Mixed lentils, rice, yogurt, ginger, green chilies, turmeric, mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, salt, oil.

Preparation: Soak and grind lentils and rice, ferment the batter, and bake it with spices and tempering.

Khichdi

Comforting rice and lentil dish cooked with mild spices.

Ingredients: Rice, split moong dal, ghee, cumin seeds, turmeric, ginger, green chilies, asafoetida, salt.

Preparation: Cook rice and lentils with spices for a comforting and nutritious dish.

Shrikhanda

Sweetened and flavored yogurt dessert.

Ingredients: Yogurt, sugar, saffron, cardamom, nuts.

Preparation: Strain yogurt, mix with sugar and flavorings, and garnish with nuts.

Sev Tameta

Tomato curry topped with crunchy sev.

Ingredients: Tomatoes, onions, green chilies, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, oil, sev.

Preparation: Cook a spicy tomato curry and top with crunchy sev just before serving.

Dhansak

Parsi dish of meat and lentils cooked with vegetables and spices.

Ingredients: Meat (mutton or chicken), mixed lentils, vegetables (eggplant, pumpkin), onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, green chilies, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, salt, oil.

Preparation: Cook meat with lentils and vegetables in a blend of spices for a hearty dish.
